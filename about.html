About Me

I’m a Master’s student in Digital and Social Media Marketing, focused on how brands communicate clearly, position themselves effectively, and engage audiences in a way that drives results.

I’m drawn to marketing for its ability to fuse strategic thinking with creativity. I’m particularly interested in understanding what makes content and campaigns perform—not just generating ideas, but shaping them into work that has a clear purpose and outcome.

Currently based in Windermere in the Lake District, I take inspiration from the outdoors, which influences how I approach content with an emphasis on clarity, simplicity, and authenticity. Having grown up in London, I’m equally comfortable in fast-paced environments and enjoy building relationships and working with a wide range of people.

I take ownership of my work and naturally step into leadership roles when needed. I value structure, accountability, and producing work to a consistently high standard.
